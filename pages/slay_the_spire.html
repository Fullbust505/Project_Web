Slay the Spire

MegaCrit — 2019

Our Review

Slay the Spire is a masterful blend of roguelike progression and deck-building strategy. Each run is a new puzzle, requiring careful planning, adaptation, and a bit of luck. The game’s replayability and depth make it a must-play for fans of strategy and card games.

Strengths

Deep and strategic deck-building mechanics
High replay value with procedurally generated runs
Unique characters and playstyles
Challenging and rewarding progression

Weaknesses

Luck can sometimes outweigh strategy
Visuals are functional but not outstanding
