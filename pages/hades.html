Hades

Supergiant Games — 2020

Our Review

Hades is an outstanding action roguelite, blending narrative, fast-paced gameplay, and stunning art direction. Every run is unique, and every death is an opportunity to learn more about the world and its colorful characters.

Strengths

  • Smooth, fast, and addictive gameplay
  • Remarkable art direction and music
  • Charming characters and dynamic dialogues
  • High replay value

Weaknesses

  • Can become repetitive for some players
  • Difficulty can be frustrating at times
