Hades

Supergiant Games — 2020

Our Review

In Hades, you play as Zagreus, the rebellious son of Hades, who is determined to escape the Underworld. The game combines fast-paced combat with a rich narrative, allowing you to interact with a wide cast of gods and mythological figures. As you progress, you unlock new weapons, abilities, and storylines, ensuring that no two runs are ever the same. The blend of action, storytelling, and progression makes Hades a standout title in the roguelite genre.

Hades is an outstanding action roguelite, blending narrative, fast-paced gameplay, and stunning art direction. Every run is unique, and every death is an opportunity to learn more about the world and its colorful characters. Start from the deepest layer of Tartarus and climb your way through Asphodel, Elysium, and beyond, facing relentless foes and powerful bosses. Will you have the guts to face your father's orders ?

Strengths

  • Smooth, fast, and addictive gameplay
  • Remarkable art direction and music
  • Charming characters and dynamic dialogues
  • High replay value
  • Curiosity highly rewarded

    • Weaknesses

    • Can become repetitive for some players
    • Difficulty can be frustrating at times
