The Team

When you took your first look on our website, you might have thought that we were a bunch working behind, didn'tcha ? Well sorry to break the myth, but we're only 2 ! However, we both prefer to stay anonymous, since this project is on a public Github repository.

We're of course fervent video game enjoyers, as we tried games ranging from visual novels to obnoxiously raging rogue-likes. But as we dove into deeper in this world, we realized that the best one we've tried come from small creators ! As a tribute to them, our website came to life.
